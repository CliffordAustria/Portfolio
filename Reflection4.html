Invitation Design Activity


How can we improve this?

It is easy to make an invitation card. Address the person you are inviting with your card, name the event you are inviting them to, spell out the date , time and place, provide a contact number so that they can know that they are in the right place. It must be simple and understandable when creating an invitational card, but it is also straight to the point as much as possible. When creating an invitational card, here are the following steps:
1st step: You should first find a template that is good for the eyes when creating an invitational card.
2nd Step: Know your attendees
3rd step: Be specific when giving a date and time so that when they show up, the participants are aware.
4th step: An address is always inserted within the invitation card so that the attendees know where they are going.
5th step: Make as simple as possible and understandable
